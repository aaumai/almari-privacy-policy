How do I rent an item?
Browse our catalog, select an item, choose your rental dates, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive confirmation and delivery details once your order is processed.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, and other secure payment methods. All payments are processed securely.
How do I return rented items?
Items are picked up by our delivery partners on the return date. Make sure items are clean and in the same condition as received.
What if an item is damaged?
Contact support immediately if you receive a damaged item. We'll arrange a replacement or refund as per our policy.
How do I become a store owner?
Register as a store owner in the app and complete the verification process. Contact us at info@aumai.co.in for more information.
How do I become a delivery partner?
Register as a delivery partner in the app and upload required documents. Contact us at info@aumai.co.in for assistance.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen. You'll receive a reset link via email.
How do I track my order?
Go to "My Orders" in the app to see all your orders and their current status. You'll receive notifications for order updates.
